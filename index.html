About

I'm Fabiano, also known as Kavis. I'm a graphic designer from San Franciso, born in Belo Horizonte, Brazil. I like working with Print and recently began to adventure myself in the web world.

I have a Bachelor degree in Advertising and I'm currently enrolled in the Design Communication Arts at UCLA Extension and training Front End Web Development.
